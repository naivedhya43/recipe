Sev Usad

A Gujarati Famous Dish

Sev Usal is a popular, spicy, and tangy street food from Vadodara, Gujarat, featuring a stew of white peas topped with crunchy sev (chickpea noodles), raw onions, coriander, and lemon juice

Ingredients

  • Mixed sprouts (matki/moth/green gram)
  • Onion (chopped)
  • Tomato (chopped)
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Green chili
  • Turmeric

Steps

  1. Heat oil, sauté onion + ginger-garlic + chili
  2. Add tomato, cook till soft
  3. Add spices + salt
  4. Add sprouts + water, cook till soft
  5. Simmer to get curry consistency
  6. Serve hot, top with sev + coriander + lemon 👍
