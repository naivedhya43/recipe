Aloo Paratha

A Desi Indian Family Dish

Aloo paratha is a popular North Indian flatbread stuffed with spiced, mashed potatoes, cooked until crispy on a griddle with ghee or oil

Ingredients

  • Boiled potatoes (mashed)
  • Green chili
  • Coriander
  • Salt
  • Red chili powder
  • Garam masala

Steps

  1. Boil, peel, and mash potatoes
  2. Mix spices, chili, coriander into potatoes
  3. Knead soft dough with atta + water
  4. Make small dough balls
  5. Stuff potato mixture inside and seal
  6. Roll gently into a flat paratha
  7. Cook on hot tawa with ghee/oil (both sides)
  8. Serve hot 👍
